Polina's guide to dog feeding

Want to start making homemade food for your dog? The basic diet a dog requires consists of Vitamins and minerals, Fibers, Oils and Fats, Carbohydrates, and Proteins. Use this guide to make sure you remember to include everything your dog requires to live a healthy and happy life.
